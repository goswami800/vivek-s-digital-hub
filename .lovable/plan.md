
# Portfolio Website for Vivek Tarale

A vibrant, energetic portfolio website showcasing Vivek Tarale as a fitness influencer, gym trainer, fitness model, and event planner.

## Design Direction
- **Vibrant & Energetic** theme with bold accent colors (fiery orange/red gradients), dynamic layouts, and high-energy feel
- Smooth scroll animations and hover effects to bring energy to the experience
- Fully responsive for mobile and desktop

## Pages & Sections (Single-Page Layout)

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-screen hero with a striking background image of Vivek
- Name, tagline ("Fitness Influencer • Gym Trainer • Model • Event Planner")
- Call-to-action button linking to contact section
- Animated text or gradient overlays for a dynamic first impression

### 2. About Section
- Brief bio about Vivek's journey and passion
- Key stats (years of experience, clients trained, events planned, etc.)
- Profile photo alongside the text

### 3. Services Section
- Four cards highlighting: **Personal Training**, **Fitness Modeling**, **Event Planning**, **Influencer/Brand Collaborations**
- Each with an icon, short description, and visual flair

### 4. Photo Gallery
- Grid-based photo gallery showcasing Vivek's best photos (gym, modeling, events)
- Lightbox/modal view for full-size photo viewing
- Category filter tabs (All, Fitness, Modeling, Events)
- You'll upload photos directly into the project

### 5. Instagram Reels & Posts Showcase
- Dedicated section displaying Instagram content
- Embedded Instagram posts and reels using Instagram embed widgets
- Grid layout mimicking the Instagram aesthetic
- Link to follow on Instagram

### 6. Testimonials
- Carousel of client/collaborator testimonials
- Name, photo placeholder, and quote for each

### 7. Contact Section
- Email address displayed prominently with a clickable mailto link
- Social media links (Instagram, YouTube, etc.)
- Background image or gradient to close the page with energy

### 8. Navigation
- Sticky top navbar with smooth scroll to each section
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Social media icons in the navbar
